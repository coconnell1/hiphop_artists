Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar was born on June 17th, 1987 in Compton, California. He is known to be one of the best lyricists, and story-tellers in all of hip-hop. His first album was released in 2010 and is titled "Overly Dedicated". Since then, he has released several albums, including "Section 80", "Good Kid, M.A.A.D City", "To Pimp a Butterfly", and "DAMN". "DAMN" is his most recent album, which was released in 2017. He is currently on tour, and his next concet is on September 14th in Denver, Colorado.
